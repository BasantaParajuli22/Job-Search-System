User Guide for Job Search Platform

1. Introduction

Purpose: This platform connects job seekers with employers, offering features such as job searching, applications, and notifications.

Key Features: Job search, applying for jobs, saving jobs, employer job postings, notifications, and user management.

2. Getting Started

User Roles:

Job Seeker: Users searching for and applying for jobs.

Employer: Companies or individuals posting jobs and reviewing applications.

Account Creation:

Choose your role.Sign up with your email, password, and other neccessary details.

Login:

Log in using your registered email and password. Check RememberMe Box to stay logged In

3. For Job Seekers

Job Search:

Search for jobs by category, keyword.

Job Application:

Click on a job posting to view details and apply directly from the job page.

Once you apply, your application cannot be withdrawn.

Saving Jobs:

Save jobs to apply later or track them easily.

Profile Management:

Update your personal information, including email, contact number, and skills.

4. For Employers

Posting Jobs:

Employers can post new jobs with detailed descriptions, qualifications, and requirements.

Managing Applications:

View and manage applications for your job postings.

Profile Management:

Update your company profile and contact information.

5. Notifications

Get notified of new job applications or updates on job postings.

6. Security Features

Password Security:

Ensure your password is strong and unique to protect your account.

7. FAQ and Troubleshooting

Visit the FAQ section for answers to common questions or contact support if needed.

8. Terms and Policies

Review the platform's terms of use and privacy policy to understand your rights and responsibilities.
