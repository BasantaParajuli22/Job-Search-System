Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Job Search System. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your personal data. By using our platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide: We collect the information you provide when you register for an account, post job listings, upload resumes, or otherwise interact with our platform. This information may include:

  • Your name, email address, username, and password.
  • Your contact information (phone number, address).
  • Your resume, job history, skills, and qualifications.
  • Information you provide in your profile or job listings.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information: When you use our platform, we may also automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Your IP address, device type, and browser information.
  • Usage data, such as the pages you view and the actions you take.
  • Cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain our platform and services.
  • To enable you to create an account, post job listings, and apply for jobs.
  • To personalize your experience on our platform.
  • To communicate with you about your account and services updates.
  • To improve our platform and develop new features.
  • To analyze usage trends and user preferences.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Sharing with Employers: If you are a job seeker, your profile and resume will be visible to employers using our platform, when you apply to a job or when they are searching for candidates.

3.2 Sharing with Other Users: Some of your profile information may be visible to other users of our platform, such as your name and profile picture, or any other information you make public.

3.3 Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us with various functions, such as payment processing, data analytics, and customer support. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

3.4 Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as a subpoena or court order.

3.5 No Sale of Personal Information: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights

5.1 Access and Correction: You have the right to access and update your personal information through your account settings.

5.2 Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and personal data. We may retain some information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

5.3 Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those emails. However, we may still send you emails about your account or service updates.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child, we will delete it promptly.
